The Opportunity

We are looking for a Senior Product Designer who believes that technology should be as beautiful as it is functional. At Lumina, we are rebuilding the creative suite for the next generation of digital artists. You will lead the design of our core canvas experience, ensuring that complex tools feel intuitive and weightless.

This isn't just about moving pixels; it's about defining the ergonomics of digital creation. You'll work closely with engineers and artists to prototype, test, and ship features that will be used by millions.

Your Experience

  • check_circle 6+ years of experience designing complex SaaS platforms or creative tools.
  • check_circle A portfolio that demonstrates a deep understanding of visual hierarchy, motion, and typography.
  • check_circle Proficiency with modern design stacks (Figma, Framer, Webflow).
  • check_circle A "sanctuary-first" approach to UX—avoiding clutter and prioritizing focus.
