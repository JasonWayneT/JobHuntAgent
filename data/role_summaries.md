My daily responsibilities revolved around unblocking internal developer bottlenecks, managing continuous platform updates, and leading voluntary, customer-centric migration tools that protected baseline revenue from the friction typically associated with forced upgrades.

This successfully eliminated customer churn stemming from stale-contact complaints and unblocked revenue-generating sales motions for our highest-tier accounts. Furthermore, I acted as the primary cross-functional bridge across a highly dynamic environment of 15+ software engineers, DevOps, DBAs, Legal, and CX leaders. I established rigorous capacity modeling and ticket prioritization disciplines, resolved a 90% security backlog consisting of approximately 300 items, and safely integrated premium content data providers like LexisNexis while ensuring strict compliance with complex third-party legal guidelines.
