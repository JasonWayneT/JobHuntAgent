### 3. Key Experience Bridge: Cision Stories to Joveo's Technical Challenges

**Joveo's Challenge:** AI-driven optimization and real-time insights require robust, clean, and reliable data pipelines. The platform needs to dynamically manage job postings and recruitment media, implying complex data ingestion, processing, and output.

**Jason's Experience Bridge:**

*   **Bellwether Initiative (Data Integrity & Pipeline Modernization):**
    *   **Story:** "At Cision, the biggest customer complaint and churn driver was stale contact data. We were losing 30-40% of data due to fragmented legacy ETL pipelines. Instead of patching, I led the Bellwether initiative to bypass the entire legacy chain, integrating directly with our upstream source of truth (GPOD). This involved a full data sync and ongoing delta processing, which eliminated the data drop-off and resolved the primary stale-data complaint. While I cannot quantify retained revenue, it directly addressed our largest churn risk."
    *   **Connection to Joveo:** "This experience directly translates to Joveo's need for high-quality data to feed its AI models. My ability to identify critical data bottlenecks, propose architectural simplifications, and execute solutions that ensure data freshness and reliability is essential for an AI-powered platform. I understand the impact of data quality on predictive analytics and user trust."

*   **CPRE Infrastructure & Stability (Platform Reliability & Cost Optimization):**
    *   **Story:** "Managing CPRE, the underlying platform for C3, meant constant work on stability and cost control. For example, our DT Search indexing component frequently caused outages due to storage exhaustion. I implemented monitoring, alerting, and a legal-vetted process to remove dead accounts, significantly reducing these outages. We also resolved approximately 90% of a 300-item security backlog through prioritized, phased remediation, and achieved $1-2M in cost savings via infrastructure optimization, even with shrinking teams."
    *   **Connection to Joveo:** "Joveo's platform, which dynamically optimizes media spending and engages candidates 24/7, requires exceptional stability and cost efficiency. My experience in maintaining critical infrastructure, proactively addressing security risks, and driving cost savings through targeted optimizations is directly applicable. I can help ensure the underlying platform is robust enough to support AI at scale, prevent disruptions, and manage operational costs effectively."

### 4. Handling Ambiguity & Stability

"Operating in environments with significant ambiguity and resource constraints has been a constant throughout my career, particularly at Cision. The platform I managed was considered legacy, faced repeated layoffs, and saw frequent leadership changes. This meant the 'right' work often wasn't new features, but rather stabilizing existing systems, protecting revenue, and enabling safe transitions.

My approach to balancing stability with innovation, especially on smaller teams, involves disciplined prioritization. I evolved from being reactive to implementing structured capacity planning, using T-shirt sizing with uncertainty, and allocating capacity across critical buckets like security, stability, and planned initiatives. This allowed us to tackle a 90% security backlog over time without halting all other work.

When faced with ambiguity, I prioritize building the systems that provide clarity. For example, when measurement systems were fragmented, I initiated discussions to improve tooling for better churn and migration visibility. My focus is on pragmatic solutions that deliver tangible outcomes, even if the path isn't perfectly clear or resources are limited. I believe this experience in navigating complexity and driving disciplined execution is highly relevant to a growth-stage company like Joveo, where priorities can shift, and resources need to be deployed strategically."
