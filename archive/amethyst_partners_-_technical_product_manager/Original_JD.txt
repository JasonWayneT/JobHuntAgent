Global leader in Alternative Investments has developed and built an Investor Portal which is widely becoming the go to platform for Fund Managers and Investors.
To continue their expansion, we are looking for a Technical Product Manager who will provide vision and execution of the platform and product.
This role is fully remote and can be based anywhere in The USA.
Reporting to and working with the CTO and CEO, we are looking for the following:
Define and own the strategic roadmap for APIs, SDKs, and integrations.
Develop long-term vision for how our integrations and platform extend value proposition to partners, clients, and internal teams.
Prioritize, plan, and drive the delivery of API and integration features — from authentication, authorization, and performance to documentation and versioning.
Ensure high quality, reliability, scalability, and usability of APIs and SDKs (for internal and external developers).
Collaborate with engineering and architecture teams to make informed architectural decisions, manage technical debt, and plan for long-term scalability.
Lead engagements with third-party vendors, partners, and clients to integrate their systems with our platform via APIs/SDKs.
Identify, evaluate, and execute integrations with external platforms (e.g., CRMs, data platforms, reporting tools, partner systems), including defining requirements, building specs, and managing delivery.
Serve as a technical liaison between company and external engineering/partner teams — helping with SDK integration guidance, authentication/authorization flows, and performance troubleshooting.
Work closely with engineering, product, operations, sales, and customer-success teams to align on roadmap, scope, delivery timelines, and launch strategy.
Work with engineering teams to ensure API documentation, SDK references, sample code, integration guides, and best practices are maintained.
Qualifications:
6+ years of product management experience, with prior experience in technical, platform, or integration-focused product roles (APIs, SDKs, third-party integrations).
