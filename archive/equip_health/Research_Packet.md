# Research Packet: Equip Health (Product Manager II, Reporting & Analytics)

## Module A: The Company DNA
- **Core Mission/Values:** To provide eating disorder treatment that works to everyone who needs it. Fully virtual, mission-driven team ("Equipsters").
- **Problem Space:** Lack of access to effective, evidence-based eating disorder care; replacing residential treatment with home-based Family-Based Treatment (FBT).
- **Business Model:** Tech-enabled, insurance-covered treatment provider using 5-person dedicated care teams.
- **Founding Story:** Founded in 2019 by Kristina Saffran and Dr. Erin Parks to scale evidence-based care they personally knew was effective but inaccessible.

## Module B: Market & Competitors
- **The "Big 3" Competitors:** 
  1. **Arise:** Virtual eating disorder treatment competitor.
  2. **The Last Food Fight:** Digital eating disorder support tool.
  3. **Shed Rx:** Online weight management and metabolic health competitor.
- **Macro Trends:** Telehealth expansion; shift toward value-based care in mental health; increasing demand for data-proven clinical outcomes.
