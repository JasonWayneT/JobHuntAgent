Carefull - Product Manager
Carefull is a fintech company focused on financial safety for older adults and their families.
About the Role:
We’re looking for a Product Manager who can own and drive aspects of the core Carefull platform and our B2B partner-facing product experience. This is a hands-on, high-impact role and part of our growing PM team. You’ll be the connective tissue between our enterprise partners, our engineering team, and our business—translating complex partner needs into clear product specs, running execution with our engineering team, and using data to inform what we build next.
This isn’t a role where you’ll be handed a roadmap. You’ll be building it—and then making sure it ships. We need someone who has done this before: managed backlogs, run sprints, defined KPIs, and delivered product in a fast-moving environment where the margin for error is small and the stakes are real.
What You’ll Do:
- Own the product roadmap for aspects of Carefull’s core Financial Safety platform and B2B partner-facing features including integrations, balancing partner requirements, business goals, and technical feasibility.
- Translate enterprise partner needs into detailed product specifications and user stories that engineering can build against with confidence.
- Run sprint ceremonies, manage the backlog, and drive day-to-day execution with our engineering leads.
- Define, track, and communicate product KPIs and success metrics.
- Conduct customer discovery and user research.
- Collaborate closely with sales to support partner conversations.
- Partner with design using tools like Figma.
- Communicate product direction, tradeoffs, and progress clearly.
Who You Are:
- 5+ years of product management experience, with a strong track record of shipping product in a B2B or B2B2C environment.
- Executor first.
- Strategic thinker.
- Experience with enterprise clients/partners.
- Experience in fintech, financial services, or a regulated industry preferred.
- Comfortable with analytics tools.
- AI-native (uses AI tools daily).
Why This Role:
Carefull is growing quickly. Flat organization. Direct work with leadership.
Remote US.
