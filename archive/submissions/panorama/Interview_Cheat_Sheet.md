# Panorama - Interview Cheat Sheet

## 1. "Tell me about yourself" (60-90 seconds)
"I am a Product Manager specializing in platform infrastructure and revenue-bearing B2B operations. For the last four years at Cision, I owned the underlying platform infrastructure for a $40M ARR enterprise suite serving 3,500 clients. My day-to-day involved working directly with SRE, DevOps, and engineering to solve systemic friction points—whether that was ripping apart legacy ETL pipelines killing our data integrity, or partnering with Legal to prioritize the remediation of 300+ SOC 2 vulnerabilities. I thrive at the intersection where infrastructure scaling meets compliance reality."

## 2. Why Panorama Platform?
"Platform scale in education technology creates zero margin for error. Managing 15 million students requires a foundational architecture that allows product teams to build fast without causing data regressions or triggering FERPA/SOC 2 incidents. I want this role because my core competency is jumping into complex technical environments, aligning SRE with product goals, and ensuring the internal tooling lets the rest of the company run seamlessly."

## 3. The Core Bridge (SRE/Security Alignment)
*   **The SRE Partnership:** "At Cision, recurring index outages were paralyzing customer searches. I partnered with SRE and engineering to implement raw storage alerting and capacity tracking, eliminating the blind spots and unlocking $1-2M in cost savings."
*   **The Data Integrity Win:** "A 30% API drop-off was our highest product churn driver. I partnered with internal teams to build a direct pipeline bypass (Project Bellwether), eliminating the drop-off entirely and restoring customer trust."
*   **The Security Reality:** "I managed the roadmap for 300 penetration test findings. You don't just 'do security'—you map it against roadmap capacity so the business doesn't stall while fixing technical debt."

## 4. Handling Ambiguity 
"When priorities shift or compliance requirements hit suddenly, I rely on rigorous risk-scoring. I implemented capacity tracking with T-shirt sizing uncertainty bands so when Engineering tells me we have an SRE fire, I can definitively show leadership the exact opportunity cost."

## 5. Custom Reverse-Interview Questions (For leadership)
1. "When product teams request new shared services, where do you currently see the most operational friction causing delays?"
2. "How mature are your current observability platforms when diagnosing latency or integrity issues across the school survey pipelines?"
3. "Is the platform roadmap currently dictated by 'keeping the lights on' tech debt, or are you actively transitioning to net-new capabilities?"
