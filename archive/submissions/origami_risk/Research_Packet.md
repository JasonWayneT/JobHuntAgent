# Research Packet: Origami Risk (v2.0)

## Module A: The Company DNA
- **Core Mission/Values:** 
  - **Stated:** Trusted data platform for the risk and insurance ecosystem; focus on business objectives and outcomes.
  - **Observed:** High degree of configurability and a partnership-heavy service model. Culture emphasizes "thinking big" and "doing what it takes to deliver."
- **Problem Space:** Centralizing and managing risk, safety, compliance, and P&C insurance data in a cloud-native platform.
- **Business Model:** SaaS-based platform with modular subscriptions. Serves corporate entities, carriers, brokers, and TPAs.
- **Founding Story:** Built as a modern, high-configurability alternative to rigid legacy RMIS (Risk Management Information Systems).

## Module B: Market & Competitors
- **The "Big 3" Competitors:** 
  1. **ServiceNow (GRC):** Broad enterprise scale.
  2. **Riskonnect:** Core integrated risk management competitor.
  3. **Archer (formerly RSA):** Deep heritage in enterprise GRC.
- **Macro Trends:** The shift toward "configurable vs. custom" and the need for unified data platforms in the insurance vertical.

## Module C: Recent Intelligence (90-Day Window)

- **Organizational Shifts:** Consistent hiring; awarded "Top Workplace" status multiple years in a row.
- **Product/Partnership News:** Continuous expansion of GRC and RMIS platform modules.
