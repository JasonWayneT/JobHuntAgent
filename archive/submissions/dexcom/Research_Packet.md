# Research Packet: Dexcom

## Module A: The Company DNA
- **Core Mission/Values:** 
  - **Stated:** To unlock information and insights that drive better health outcomes and improve human health.
  - **Observed:** Shifting from a "diabetes monitoring" company to a "metabolic health intelligence" company. Values "empowering people to take control of health" through biowearables (CGM).
- **Problem Space:** Chronic disease management (diabetes) relies on continuous, accurate data. Dexcom provides biosensing technology to move from reactive fingersticks to proactive, personalized health insights.
- **Business Model:** Public MedTech leader (NASDAQ: DXCM). Revenue from hardware sales (sensors/transmitters) and recurring digital subscriptions. Transitioning into the OTC consumer health market with "Stelo."
- **Founding Story:** Founded 25 years ago as a pioneer in continuous glucose monitoring. Evolved from an industry underdog to the global standard for interoperable CGM.

## Module B: Market & Competitors

- **Macro Trends:** The move toward "Metabolic Health for All"—expanding CGM use beyond Type 1 diabetics to 30M+ Type 2 patients and athletes.
