# Research Packet: Pluralsight (v2.0)

## Module A: The Company DNA
- **Core Mission/Values:** 
  - **Stated:** Help customers improve learning and business outcomes via frictionless user engagement and tech skills development.
  - **Observed:** Shift toward API-first content tools, emphasizing headless authoring, labs, and interactive assessments.
- **Problem Space:** Scaling edtech content creation (courses, assessments, labs) across global channels and multi-persona authors.
- **Business Model:** SaaS subscription-based enterprise learning. Mid-stage growth with a focus on platform scaling and API-first distribution.
- **Founding Story:** Established edtech leader transforming from a traditional course library into a modern "Skills Progress" platform.

## Module B: Market & Competitors
- **The "Big 3" Competitors:** 
  1. **Udemy Business:** Large marketplace-driven content.
  2. **LinkedIn Learning:** Integrated with the professional social graph.
  3. **Coursera for Business:** Strong academic and certification focus.
- **Macro Trends:** The move toward "agentic" learning AI (MCP integrations) and the need for localized, accessible, and interactive content (Labs/Sandboxes).
