# Research Packet: Dropbox (v2.0)

## Module A: The Company DNA
- **Core Mission/Values:** 
  - **Stated:** Envisioning and building the future of work to make it more intuitive and joyful.
  - **Observed:** Aggressive pivot toward AI-powered experiences (universal search, agentic workflows, automation) to transition from a "file sync and share" utility to an "AI productivity" platform.
- **Problem Space:** Eliminating the friction in finding, organizing, and collaborating on content across fragmented digital environments.
- **Business Model:** Mature public company ($2B+ ARR) with a subscription-based model. Operating in a "Virtual First" remote environment across US time zones.
- **Founding Story:** Established as a platform-agnostic cloud storage pioneer; now scaling via AI to compete in the next generation of productivity tools.
