Company: Daktronics
About the job

We are seeking a strategic and execution-focused Product Manager to drive the success of SaaS software in a fast-paced, Agile environment. In this role, you will own the product vision, strategy, and lifecycle for cloud-based applications with shared data platforms and external integrations. 

Your Responsibilities Will Include:
Stakeholder Collaboration: Collaborate with business leaders, partners, customers, technical teams, and other stakeholders to ensure product success. 
Product Vision and Strategy: Develop and articulate a clear product vision and strategy that aligns with the company's goals and objectives. 
Lifecycle: Own the product through its full lifecycle.

Where is this opportunity located? 
Daktronics offers the flexibility to work from a U.S. based home office or Daktronics U.S. location. 

Qualifications:
Bachelor’s degree.
5+ years of software product management experience.
Management of SaaS products, shared data platforms, external product integrations and external partnerships. 
The typical hiring range for this position is $100,000 to $135,000 annually.
