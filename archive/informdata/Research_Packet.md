# Research Packet: InformData (v2.0)
## Role: Product Manager, Criminal Operations

### Module A: The Company DNA
- **Core Mission/Values:**
  - **Stated:** "Shaping the landscape of workforce management." Focus on empowering organizations with better data.
  - **Observed:** Transitioning from traditional background checks to a high-tech "Data as a Service" model for the criminal operations space.
- **Problem Space:** Accuracy and speed in criminal record checks are legally mandated and operationally difficult. InformData provides the "System of Record" that ensures background screenings are accurate and timely.
- **Business Model:** B2B Data Services (DaaS) for the background screening and risk management industry. Private Equity backed.
- **Founding Story:** A result of the merger between SJV Data Solutions and Direct-to-Source, aiming to create the industry's most direct and accurate data provider.

### Module B: Market & Competitors
- **The "Big 3" Competitors:**
  - **First Advantage:** Market leader in global background check and drug screening solutions.
  - **Checkr:** Modern, API-first competitor that popularization "Automated" background checks.
  - **Sterling:** Another large-scale institutional player in the identity and background verification space.
- **InformData Differentiator:** Specialized "Direct Source" data. Unlike competitors who may rely on aggregated or secondary databases, InformData focuses on direct court-house and primary source acquisition.
- **Macro Trends:** The move toward "Real-Time Continuous Monitoring." Companies want to know about criminal activity when it happens, not just during pre-employment.
