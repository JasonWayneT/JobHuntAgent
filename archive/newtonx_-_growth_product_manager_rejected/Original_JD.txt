Company: NewtonX
Title: Growth Product Manager

About NewtonX
NewtonX is the leading B2B market research provider that helps businesses make informed decisions by connecting them with verified subject matter experts.

About The Role
We are seeking a dynamic Growth Product Manager to lead the development of growth-focused products that accelerate the acquisition, activation, and early retention of our expert community (NXC). In this role, you will own the expert growth funnel end-to-end —including the ability for the business to power it’s research function – working at the intersection of product, data, marketing, and operations to identify opportunities, define and deliver growth initiatives, and establish a rigorous experimentation framework.

How You Will Make An Impact
* Strategic Roadmapping & Leadership Visibility - Shape and maintain the product-led growth roadmap across the entire business.
* Drive Growth Strategy - Own the expert acquisition and activation funnel end-to-end.
* Experimentation & Learning - Adopt a hypothesis-driven approach to product-led growth. Run A/B tests, evaluate funnel performance, and rapidly iterate.
* Market & Audience Insights - Partner with Data Analytics to analyze client demand trends.
* Channel & Campaign Optimization - Collaborate closely with the Marketing team.
* Onboarding & Engagement Flow - Own and optimize the onboarding and sign-up experience.
* Cross-Functional Collaboration - Work closely with Engineering, Product Design, Marketing, and Operations.
* Performance Tracking & Analytics - Define, monitor, and report on key KPIs.

Who You Are
* Experience: 5+ years in Product Management, ideally with exposure to Growth, PLG, or marketplace dynamics.
* Data-Driven: Skilled at interpreting funnel data, measuring outcomes, and using analytics to drive decisions.
* Experimentation Mindset: Experience designing, running, and scaling A/B tests or growth experiments.
* Collaboration: Proven success working across functions (Marketing, Design, Engineering, Ops) to deliver results.
* Adaptability: Thrives in fast-paced, iterative environments.
