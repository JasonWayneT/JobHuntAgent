Company: QuinStreet
Title: Senior Associate Product Manager

Corporate Overview
QuinStreet is a pioneer in powering decentralized online marketplaces that match searchers and “research and compare” consumers with brands. We run these virtual- and private-label marketplaces in one of the nation’s largest media networks.
Our campaign-results-driven matching decision engines and optimization algorithms are built from over 20 years and billions of dollars of online media experience.

Job Category
We’re building and scaling a high-impact consumer fintech marketplace that connects millions of users with financial products tailored to their needs. As a Senior Associate Product Manager, you’ll own critical parts of the user journey—from initial onboarding to offer experiences—and help drive improvements in conversion, engagement, and revenue.

Responsibility
- Drive experimentation across core product surfaces (form flow, offer pages, monetization experiences)
- Analyze user behavior and performance metrics (conversion rates, engagement, revenue per user) to identify opportunities
- Partner with Design to simplify and improve decision-making experiences for users
- Collaborate with Engineering to scope, build, and launch high-impact features
- Support expansion into new product verticals and monetization strategies

Requirements
- 4+ years of experience in product management, analytics, consulting, or a related role
- Strong analytical thinking and comfort working with data to inform decisions
- Demonstrated ability to take ownership and drive projects from idea to execution
- Experience working cross-functionally with technical and non-technical teams
- Curiosity and interest in fintech, marketplaces, or growth-focused product work

Salary Range: $90,000 - $100,000.
