Key Responsibilities:
Define and own the product vision, roadmap, and strategy.
Translate complex user workflows into simple and scalable product experiences.
Work closely with engineering and design teams to deliver high-impact features.
Engage with customers to understand needs and gather product insights.

Qualifications:
7+ years of experience in product management or product leadership.
Experience building SaaS platforms or workflow software.
Strong ability to work cross-functionally with engineering and design teams.
