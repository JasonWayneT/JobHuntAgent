# Coinbase - Product Manager, Enterprise Productivity

Drive Coinbase’s work execution and collaboration platforms portfolio—including Jira, Linear, Asana, Slack, and emerging tools—and to lead the optimization of how work is planned, communicated, tracked, and delivered across Coinbase. Define the strategy, roadmap, and governance for this ecosystem, ensuring our task and communication platforms operate as a cohesive, scalable system.
Requirements:
- Proven experience owning or driving a portfolio of work management and/or collaboration platforms (e.g., Jira, Linear, Asana, Slack).
- Track record of driving measurable improvements in team productivity, cycle time, delivery predictability, or collaboration health.
- Experience working with diverse personas (engineers, designers, product managers, operations, support).
