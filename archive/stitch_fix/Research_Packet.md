# Research Packet: Stitch Fix (Product Manager, Supply Chain & Operations Systems)

## Module A: The Company DNA
- **Core Mission/Values:** Changing the way people find clothes they love by combining data science with personal styling.
- **Problem Space:** Inefficiencies in traditional online shopping; reducing "choice overload" through algorithmic and human curation.
- **Business Model:** Curated "Fix" boxes (subscription/on-demand) and "Freestyle" direct shopping.
- **Founding Story:** Founded in 2011 by Katrina Lake to revolutionize retail through personalization.

## Module B: Market & Competitors
- **The "Big 3" Competitors:** 
  1. **Wantable/Dia & Co:** Niche subscription competitors.
  2. **Amazon Fashion:** Scale and logistics competitor.
  3. **Nordstrom/Trunk Club:** High-end service competitor.
- **Macro Trends:** Shift towards profitability over hyper-growth; focus on supply chain efficiency and inventory turnover.
