Summary

Role: Product Manager

Location: Remote 

Duration: 6+ Months

 

We are seeking an experienced Product Manager with strong expertise in the Healthcare domain to lead product strategy and execution.
The ideal candidate will drive end-to-end product lifecycle management from ideation to launch and continuous improvement.
Must have hands-on experience working with Agile tools such as Jira and Confluence for backlog management and documentation.
Proficiency in Azure DevOps is required to manage sprint planning, tracking, and release workflows.
Strong understanding of version control systems, particularly Git.
Experience working with CI/CD tools and pipelines to enable automated build, test, and deployment processes.
Collaborate closely with engineering, QA, UX, and healthcare stakeholders to define product requirements and user stories.
Analyze healthcare market trends, regulatory requirements, and customer needs to shape product vision and roadmap.
Excellent communication, stakeholder management, and data-driven decision-making skills are essential.
Bachelor’s degree in Business, Healthcare, Engineering, or related field; relevant certifications are a plus.

Key skills: Product Manager, Health care, Git Jira, Confluence.
