# Research Packet: Housecall Pro (Product Manager II - Mobile)

## Module A: The Company DNA
- **Core Mission/Values:** To champion home service pros to success. Stated values include "Relentless Innovation," "People Matter," and "Operate with Discipline and Rigor."
- **Problem Space:** Solving "operational chaos" for home service professionals (plumbers, HVAC, etc.) who traditionally rely on manual or fragmented systems.
- **Business Model:** B2B SaaS platform consolidating scheduling, dispatching, invoicing, payments, and customer management.
- **Founding Story:** Founded in 2013 to help pros like the founder's father save time and grow their businesses through technology.

## Module B: Market & Competitors
- **The "Big 3" Competitors:** 
  1. **ServiceTitan:** Heavy hitter for large/complex enterprises.
  2. **Jobber:** Direct rival for mid-market/smaller service businesses.
  3. **Workiz:** Strong focus on scheduling and dispatching.
- **Micro Trends:** Increasing demand for mobile-first operational tools, embedded financial services (payments), and AI-driven automation in field services.
