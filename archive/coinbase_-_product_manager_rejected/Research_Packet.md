# Research Packet: Coinbase (Product Manager I, Growth Engagement)

## Module A: The Company DNA
- **Core Mission/Values:** To increase economic freedom in the world. High-performance "championship team" culture. Mission-first.
- **Problem Space:** Democratizing access to financial services through a secure, trusted, and easy-to-use crypto-financial ecosystem.
- **Business Model:** Multi-faceted revenue from transaction fees, subscriptions (Coinbase One), staking, and infrastructure (Base, USDC).
- **Founding Story:** Founded in 2012 by Brian Armstrong and Fred Ehrsam to make Bitcoin accessible to everyone.
