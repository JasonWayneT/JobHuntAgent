Company: Snapsheet Inc
Title: Product Manager I - Foundational Services

- Negotiate acceptance criteria and find the right balance of scope.
- Rigorously leverage data and analytics to drive decisions.

Qualifications:
- 3-5+ years of experience and success in B2B/SaaS/Web.
- Demonstrated experience as a technical product manager or working with an API-based product.
- Experience leading substantial aspects of product management.
- A data-driven approach to scope and priority management.
- Exceptional communication & organization skills.
