# Research Packet: Snapsheet Inc (v2.0)
## Role: Product Manager I - Foundational Services

### Module A: The Company DNA
- **Core Mission/Values:**
  - **Stated:** "Claims technology the way it should be: purposeful, precise, and designed to deliver outcomes."
  - **Observed:** Focus on "open architecture" that fits into existing carrier workflows rather than forcing a proprietary silo.
- **Problem Space:** P&C insurance claims are traditionally slow, manual, and paper-heavy. Snapsheet provides the "Cloud-based claims management" infrastructure to digitize the entire lifecycle.
- **Business Model:** B2B SaaS for the insurance industry. Trusted by 170+ carriers and logistics companies.
- **Founding Story:** Founded in 2011; pioneered virtual vehicle appraisals and expanded into a full-scale claims operating system.

### Module B: Market & Competitors
- **The "Big 3" Competitors:**
  - **Guidewire:** The legacy enterprise elephant in the room for insurance software.
  - **Duck Creek:** Another major provider of core systems for P&C carriers.
  - **CCC Intelligent Solutions:** Strong competitor in the collision and claims space.
- **Snapsheet Differentiator:** "Purposeful precision." Unlike legacy systems, Snapsheet is built with an "API-first" and "Open Architecture" mindset, allowing faster integration.
- **Macro Trends:** The "Digital First" mandate in insurance. Carriers are under pressure to reduce "Cycle Time" (time to settle a claim) using automation and AI.
