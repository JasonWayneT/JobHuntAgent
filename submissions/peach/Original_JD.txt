Company: Peach
About the job

About Peach:
Peach is a modern loan management and servicing platform empowering lenders to launch and scale new lending programs. We provide a fully integrated, configurable system of record with API-first architecture, enabling lenders to bring products to market quickly while maintaining full compliance and operational efficiency.

About The Role:
We’re looking for a Product Manager to lead the next generation of financial products and features at Peach. This is a cross-functional role focused on building and scaling lending capabilities within servicing, collections, and compliance workflows. 

What You'll Do:
Translate operational workflows and lender pain points into clear feature requirements and measurable outcomes.
Lead cross-functional teams (engineering, data, operations, legal/compliance) to design, build, and ship features.
Work closely with legal and compliance teams to ensure all solutions meet regulatory, audit, and explainability standards.

Qualifications:
5+ years of Product Management experience, ideally in fintech, credit, lending, payments, or similar regulated domains.
Track record of leading cross-functional teams and shipping in regulated environments.
Excellent written and verbal communication skills across technical and business audiences.
The pay range for this role is 130,000 - 145,000 USD per year (Remote)
