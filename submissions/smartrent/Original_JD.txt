Who We Are




SmartRent (NYSE: SMRT) is revolutionizing how people live and work with the industry’s only end-to-end platform designed for the rental housing industry. By uniting purpose-built software, integrated hardware and full implementation and support in one ecosystem, we help owners and operators simplify operations, cut costs and deliver exceptional resident experiences. Recognized by Deloitte, HousingWire and the PropTech Breakthrough Awards, SmartRent is shaping the future of property technology and redefining what it means to make rental housing smarter.

Job Description




As a SmartRent Product Manager, you will be involved in every aspect of the product development lifecycle, from formulating the next innovative idea to working directly with end users, designers, engineers and stakeholders to implement key feature enhancements. You will be expected to push the boundaries of what’s possible, while sharing our healthy obsession for quality and attention to detail. You will lead the ideation, technical documentation, and launch of product features that provide the best-in-class solutions. You will take a deep dive and learn the core business so you can empathize with our customers and end users to continuously improve the experience.




Responsibilities





Drive product development from existing feature enhancements to entirely new epics


Collect and translate business requirements into detailed technical documentation containing thorough acceptance criteria


Work hands-on with designers to constantly iterate and improve the UI/UX


Attend scrums, retrospectives and planning meetings while taking detailed notes with clearly defined action items


Participate in agile activities such as backlog grooming, feature prioritization and sprint planning


Define and ensure user acceptance and testing criteria is captured and followed throughout the QA


Maintain a direct line of communication with power users


Work closely with support, operations and sales to understand existing friction points


Aggregate feedback from all channels to drive priority


Create exciting release notes for internal teams and end users


Build and maintain user personas, journey maps and product roadmaps


Assist our design team in the creation of product prototypes, wireframes and mockups


Maintain internal training tools, user guides and knowledge base articles


Communicate effectively with all internal teams and external entities teams to set accurate expectations and proper timelines for product delivery





Required Qualifications





Bachelor’s Degree in Computer Science, Engineering, Business or related technical degree


2+ years of experience as a Product Manager, Business Systems Analyst and/or Jr. Product Manager capacity


Highly analytical and diligent personality with ability to constantly solve problems and quickly plan out new features


Expertise in researching, defining and documenting complex business processes


Ability to understand customer needs, business goals and competitive pressures with the ability to convert those into technical documents.


High aptitude to proactively develop customer relationships by empathizing, understanding, anticipating, and providing solutions to customer needs.


Experience defining a product roadmap and prioritizing features based on end user needs


Successful track record of collaboration with key stakeholders


Excellent communication and presentation skills


Proficient with common productivity and collaboration tools


Familiarity with JIRA, Confluence, Figma, Sketch, Github, Prototyping


Must be passionate about the business with a constant desire to learn more and improve the user experience!





Preferred Qualifications





Experience in Multifamily residential housing industry


Experience working on SaaS (Software-as-a-Service) products
