OneDigital - Embedded Product Manager
OneDigital is an employee benefits and insurance brokerage firm.
About the Role:
The Embedded Product Manager will sit directly within a OneDigital practice area and act as a product builder responsible for rapidly identifying opportunities, shaping solutions, and bringing them to life. Leverages modern AI tools including AI agents.
What You’ll Do:
- Embed within a business practice to understand workflows.
- Identify and prioritize high-impact product opportunities.
- Build lightweight roadmaps and backlogs.
- Rapidly prototype solutions using AI tools and AI agents.
- Partner with AI builders and engineers to turn prototypes into production-ready solutions.
- Drive adoption of delivered solutions.
Who You Are:
- ~2–5 years in product management, product ownership, or consulting.
- Strong product instincts.
- Exceptional communicator.
- Builder mindset.
- Experience using AI tools.
- Familiarity with CRM systems (Salesforce, Dynamics 365) is a plus.
Remote US.
