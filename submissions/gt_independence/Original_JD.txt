About GT Independence

GT Independence is a national leader in Financial Management Services (FMS), supporting self-directed long‑term care for people of all ages and abilities. We help people hire caregivers they trust, stay in their homes, and live independently by taking on the complex administrative tasks like payroll, taxes, onboarding, and compliance that make self-direction possible. Our technology powers enrollment, EVV, payroll, reporting, and secure communication for more than 55,000 individuals across 18 states and D.C.

We believe people are the experts in their own lives. Our products exist to make self-direction easy, accessible, and empowering.

Position Summary

The Product Owner will own the strategy, roadmap, and delivery of digital solutions that simplify the self-direction experience for participants, caregivers, care coordinators, and internal operational teams. You will translate business needs and regulatory requirements into intuitive, scalable, and secure products that support GT’s mission: helping people live the lives they choose.

This role is perfect for someone who blends customer empathy with strong analytical and technical skills and who thrives in a mission-driven environment.

Key Responsibilities

Product Vision & Roadmap

Define and communicate a clear product vision aligned with GT Independence’s mission and organizational priorities.
Build and maintain a strategic product roadmap that drives improved experiences across enrollment, onboarding, payroll, EVV, and ongoing self-direction tasks.
Prioritize features using customer insights, regulatory requirements, business value, and operational efficiency.

User & Stakeholder Collaboration

Engage with participants, caregivers, internal users, and state partners to deeply understand workflows, pain points, and opportunities for improvement.
Act as the primary bridge between business stakeholders and engineering teams, ensuring alignment, clarity, and shared expectations.
Translate complex workflows and program rules into clear user stories and actionable product requirements.

Execution & Delivery

Maintain a healthy, prioritized product backlog; write detailed requirements and acceptance criteria.
Lead sprint ceremonies with engineering partners, ensuring high-quality delivery and predictable progress.
Validate product enhancements through testing, feedback cycles, and data-driven evaluation.

Continuous Improvement

Monitor product performance using analytics, feedback, and operational metrics.
Identify areas for workflow automation, process simplification, or technology modernization.
Proactively recommend enhancements that reduce administrative burden for people who self-direct and improve internal team efficiency.

Compliance, Security & Quality

Ensure product design complies with Medicaid, HCBS, EVV, and state-specific self-direction policies.
Partner with compliance and security teams to uphold GT Independence’s standards for data protection and accessible, inclusive design.
Support continuous alignment with HITRUST and other industry-leading certifications.
