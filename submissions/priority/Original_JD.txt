Title: Product Manager (ERP/Cloud Solutions)

About the Role
Act as a point of contact across banking and treasury products.
Contribute to the evolution of orchestration capabilities, including decisioning across payment rails.
Document routing logic, tradeoffs (speed, cost, risk), and support prioritization of enhancements.
Track customer pain points and requests; drive investigation, file detailed bugs, and ensure resolution.
Prioritize backlog across new features, bugs, and customer requests while balancing technical debt and customer impact.
Evaluate internal capabilities and external vendor solutions.

What Success Looks Like:
- Delivery of high-impact API enhancements.
- Reduction in customer-reported issues.
- Clear, well-defined product requirements.
- Strong collaboration with engineering, compliance, and business stakeholders.

Candidate Requirements:
- Experience in product management, preferably within fintech, banking, or payments.
- Familiarity with payment systems such as RTP, FedNow, push-to-card, or wires.
- Experience working with APIs and understanding concepts such as idempotency.
- Strong communication skills.
- Ability to navigate ambiguity and make pragmatic, well-reasoned decisions.

Compensation: $92,000 - $125,000.
