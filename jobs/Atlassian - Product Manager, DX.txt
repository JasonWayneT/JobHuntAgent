Company: Atlassian
Title: Product Manager, DX

Atlassian is looking for a Product Manager to join our Developer Experience (DX) team. You will be responsible for building products that help developers be more productive and happy.

Responsibilities:
* Define and execute the product strategy for the Developer Experience team.
* Work closely with engineering, design, and research to build and launch new products and features.
* Conduct user research and data analysis to understand the needs of developers and measure the success of your products.
* Communicate the product vision and strategy to stakeholders across the company.

Qualifications:
* 3+ years of experience in product management, preferably in a developer-focused role.
* Strong analytical skills and the ability to use data to drive product decisions.
* Excellent communication and collaboration skills.
* Passion for developer experience and productivity.
* Experience with cloud-native technologies is a plus.
