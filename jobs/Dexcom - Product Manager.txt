The Company
Dexcom Corporation (NASDAQ DXCM) is a pioneer and global leader in continuous glucose monitoring (CGM). Dexcom began as a small company with a big dream: To forever change how diabetes is managed. To unlock information and insights that drive better health outcomes. Here we are 25 years later, having pioneered an industry. And we're just getting started. We are broadening our vision beyond diabetes to empower people to take control of health. That means personalized, actionable insights aimed at solving important health challenges. To continue what we've started: Improving human health.

We are driven by thousands of ambitious, passionate people worldwide who are willing to fight like warriors to earn the trust of our customers by listening, serving with integrity, thinking big, and being dependable. We've already changed millions of lives and we're ready to change millions more. Our future ambition is to become a leading consumer health technology company while continuing to develop solutions for serious health conditions. We'll get there by constantly reinventing unique biosensing-technology experiences. Though we've come a long way from our small company days, our dreams are bigger than ever. The opportunity to improve health on a global scale stands before us.

Meet the team: 
Join Dexcom’s expanding Clinical Product team, the group responsible for building the shared services, data pipelines, and integration frameworks that power Dexcom’s connected ecosystem. We enable seamless, secure, and scalable experiences across mobile apps, cloud services, EHR integrations, partner platforms, and next generation biosensor technology. 

In this role, you will contribute to platform capabilities that support multiple Dexcom products—including clinical tools, partner integrations, connected software experiences, and global data services, ensuring our foundation is reliable, interoperable, and ready for future innovation. 

Where you come in:  
As a Product Manager, you will own platform level capabilities that enable reliable data flows, high availability services, and interoperable solutions across Dexcom’s ecosystem, with particular attention to our healthcare provider customers. While your work will influence the evolution of Dexcom’s therapy management tools, your focus is on the underlying capabilities, services, and integration frameworks that power these experiences. 

You will collaborate with engineering, architecture, clinical, and commercial teams to define and deliver enhancements to Dexcom’s shared platform, driving interoperability, service reliability, API modernization, and cross product scalability. 

Scope of Responsibility 
Define and manage the roadmap for platform services, including data pipelines, reporting engines, interoperability modules, and shared integration frameworks.  
Establish requirements for scalable, secure, and globally compliant platform capabilities, including HIPAA, GDPR, regional data residency, and regulatory expectations.  
Partner with Platform & Services teams to evolve shared components such as analytics, workflow engines, partner facing APIs, and reusable data services.  
Ensure Dexcom’s ecosystem aligns with industry interoperability standards including HL7, FHIR, SMART on FHIR, and clinical integration patterns.  
You will promote architectural patterns, design standards, and reusable services that improve cross product consistency. Contribute to platform reliability, performance, observability, and service level expectations. 

Key Responsibilities 
Translate platform strategy into clear technical requirements and user stories.  
Partner with engineering and architecture teams to shape solution designs and ensure alignment with long term platform goals.  
Help define and manage API and service level requirements, focusing on clear API requirements and lifecycle expectations.  
Collaborate with internal product teams to support adoption of shared platform capabilities and conduct research to guide interoperability and modernization efforts.  
Work cross functionally with clinical, regulatory, and commercial partners to ensure platform readiness for real world needs.  
Provide subject matter expertise for partner integrations and monitor platform performance to drive continuous improvement. 

What makes you successful:  
5-10 years of Product Management experience, preferably in regulated medical technology 
Experience managing therapy management software 
Experience working within or alongside regulated product development processes 
Strong communication and collaboration skills across clinical, technical, and design teams. 
Ability to balance user needs, clinical requirements, and business goals in a fast-paced environment. 
Familiarity with tools like Jira, Figma, and product analytics platforms is a plus. 
Clinical experience is a plus.
