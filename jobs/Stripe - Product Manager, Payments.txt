Company: Stripe
Title: Product Manager, Payments

Stripe connects merchants and financial partners globally, processing billions of transactions. As a Product Manager on the Payments team, you’ll own core pieces of Stripe’s global payment infrastructure.

In This Role, You Will
- Build deep user empathy with both Stripe's merchants and financial partners.
- Collaborate with engineering, design, legal, and other teams.
- Ensure we are building a reliable, performant platform, including system reliability, API extensibility, and latency.
- Use metrics to inform your point of view.

Minimum Requirements
- 7-12+ years of industry product management experience.
- Experience in payments, fintech, financial infrastructure, or a closely adjacent domain.
- Demonstrated experience partnering closely with engineers to build products and complex systems at scale.
- Strong analytical capabilities with experience defining and tracking success metrics.
- Background delivering successfully on multi-quarter roadmaps.
