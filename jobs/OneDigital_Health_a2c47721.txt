Our Newest Opportunity: 

The Embedded Product Manager will sit directly within a OneDigital practice area (e.g., Benefits, Retirement/Wealth, HR) and act as a product builder responsible for rapidly identifying opportunities, shaping solutions, and bringing them to life.

This is not a traditional product management role. This individual will be expected to move quickly from idea → prototype → production by leveraging modern AI tools — including AI agents — and working closely with AI builders and engineers. The role requires a strong product mindset, exceptional communication skills, and a bias toward action and experimentation.

What You’ll Do:

Embed within a business practice to deeply understand workflows, pain points, and opportunities

Quickly identify and prioritize high-impact product opportunities aligned to business goals

Build and maintain lightweight roadmaps and backlogs focused on speed and value delivery

Rapidly prototype solutions using AI tools, including AI agents, and low-code/no-code platforms

Design and collaborate on AI agent-driven solutions that automate workflows and enhance user experiences

Partner with AI builders and engineers to turn prototypes into production-ready solutions

Iterate quickly based on user feedback, data, and stakeholder input

Act as the bridge between business stakeholders and technical teams — translating needs into solutions and outcomes

Communicate clearly and frequently with stakeholders on progress, tradeoffs, and outcomes

Drive adoption of delivered solutions within the practice

Adapt across multiple domains and problem spaces as business needs evolve

What We’re Looking For:

Strong product instincts — ability to identify problems worth solving and shape effective solutions

Exceptional communicator — able to work seamlessly with both business leaders and technical teams (critical for success)

Demonstrated ability to move quickly from concept to prototype

Experience using AI tools, including AI agents, to build or accelerate product development

Comfort working in ambiguous environments with minimal structure

Builder mindset — not just defining work, but actively helping create solutions

Ability to simplify complex problems and drive toward execution

Familiarity with CRM systems (e.g. Salesforce, Dynamics 365) and understanding of data flows is a plus

Experience or exposure to financial services, retirement/wealth, or similar industries is a plus

Ability to adapt and operate across different practice areas

Experience:

~2–5 years in product management, product ownership, consulting, or similar role

Experience in fast-paced, iterative environments (startups, innovation teams, or digital product teams preferred)

Hands-on experience with AI-enabled products, particularly AI agents or automation-driven solutions, strongly preferred

Bachelor’s degree preferred, but not required

How This Role is Different:

Less focus on process, more focus on speed and outcomes

Expected to prototype and validate ideas before formal development

Works embedded in the business, not separated as a centralized product function

Leverages AI agents to automate workflows and accelerate both discovery and delivery
