About Vitestro

Founded in 2017 in Utrecht, Vitestro is pioneering the future of blood collection with the Aletta® Autonomous Robotic Phlebotomy Device™ (ARPD™). This groundbreaking medical device combines advanced multi-modal imaging (near-infrared, ultrasound, and Doppler ultrasound) with robotics and AI to perform the entire diagnostic blood draw procedure autonomously.

By addressing critical healthcare staffing shortages and improving patient experience, Vitestro is transforming one of the most common and essential medical procedures. With more than 90 team members and growing rapidly, we are scaling our impact.

At Vitestro, we are committed to continuous innovation and improvement.

 

Join us as our new Technical Product Owner (Platform & Connectivity)

As our Technical Product Owner for Platform & Connectivity, you take ownership of the cloud and connectivity layer that enables Vitestro’s products to operate reliably, securely, and at scale in clinical environments.

You operate at the intersection of engineering and product, ensuring that complex platform, data, and connectivity challenges are translated into clear priorities and delivered effectively. You are responsible for backlog ownership, prioritization, and delivery, while acting as a sparring partner for engineers and aligning stakeholders across the organization.

You work on topics such as cloud platform architecture, device connectivity, data flows, observability, and security ensuring that systems integrate seamlessly and perform reliably in real-world healthcare environments.

 

First Months at Vitestro

In your first months, your focus will be on taking ownership of the Platform & Connectivity backlog and bringing structure to ongoing work across cloud, connectivity, and data initiatives.

You align closely with engineering, product management, and key stakeholders to clarify priorities, define scope, and ensure that delivery is predictable and aligned with company goals. You facilitate refinements, define clear requirements, and ensure that engineers are working on the highest-value topics.

You build a strong understanding of the system landscape, including cloud services, data flows, device connectivity, and integrations, and quickly become a trusted sparring partner for the team.

At the same time, you help improve how work is organized, ensuring clarity in ownership, priorities, and delivery timelines across multiple complex initiatives.

 

This is what your success looks like

After 12 months, the Platform & Connectivity domain operates with clear priorities, structured backlog management, and predictable delivery.

The team delivers reliably on key initiatives related to cloud, connectivity, and data, and stakeholders have clear visibility into progress, risks, and priorities. You are recognized as the go-to person for aligning technical and product decisions within this domain.

You have built strong trust with engineering and stakeholders, and you are able to balance technical complexity with business impact, ensuring that the right decisions are made at the right time.
