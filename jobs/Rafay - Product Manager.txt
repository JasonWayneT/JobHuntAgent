Company: Rafay
Title: Product Manager

ABOUT THE ROLE
Product Managers at Rafay have the opportunity to define the future of our GPU PaaS and Cloud-Native Automation platform, empowering cloud providers and enterprise platform teams to securely deliver self-service AI and cloud-native applications at scale.

WHAT YOU WILL BE DOING
* Clearly articulate customer challenges and advocate for their needs internally
* Collaborate with third-party partners and vendors to deliver seamless, first-class user experiences for customers
* Translate customer requirements into product direction and champion the roadmap with engineering
* Embrace rapid iteration, seek continuous feedback, and drive progress toward Rafay’s vision
* Lead enablement sessions and create content to promote new features internally and externally
* Represent Rafay at trade shows and deliver technical talks at conferences and community events

WHAT WE WANT TO SEE
* 4+ years of experience in product management
* Experience in B2B SaaS and/or developer tools preferred
* Strong technical foundation with the ability to quickly ramp on new technologies
* Skilled at delivering clear, effective technical presentations
* Excellent written, verbal, and interpersonal communication skills
* Self-driven, pragmatic, and comfortable operating independently
* Growth-oriented and receptive to feedback
* Strong bias for action
