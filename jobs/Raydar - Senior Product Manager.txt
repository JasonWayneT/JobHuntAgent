Company: Raydar (Recruiter Post)
Title: Senior Product Manager (Underwriting Platform)

We are a recruitment partner supporting a fast-growing AI-driven technology company in the insurance space that is looking for a Senior Product Manager to help expand and evolve its flagship underwriting platform.

About the Role
As a Senior Product Manager, you will lead the roadmap and strategy for a core underwriting platform used by insurance teams. You’ll work closely with engineering, design, research, sales, and marketing to deliver impactful product features that solve real customer challenges.

What You’ll Do
* Own and expand the roadmap for a core Underwriting Platform, including workflows, actions, forms, and rating capabilities
* Lead product discovery, identifying user problems and translating insights into product requirements and user stories
* Drive the development of AI-powered workflow capabilities to improve customer productivity and time-to-value
* Collaborate with cross-functional teams including engineering, design, sales, and marketing on product strategy and go-to-market initiatives
* Develop a strong understanding of the insurance ecosystem to identify opportunities and build customer-centric solutions

What We’re Looking For
* 5–8 years of product management experience
* Proven experience building and shipping customer-facing B2B SaaS products
* Strong product discovery and roadmap ownership experience
* Interest in AI-powered products and workflow automation
* Experience in insurance, fintech, or complex enterprise platforms is a strong plus.
