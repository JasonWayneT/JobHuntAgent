Company: Federal Express Corporation
Title: Technical Product Manager III

Essential Functions
- Drives backlog prioritization, feature refinement, and cross-functional execution for the Commercial Data & Insights Platform.
- Responsible for managing webhook and API-driven products, working with engineering teams, multiple Product Managers, and stakeholders.
- Prioritize features and technical debt, ensuring smooth execution in iteration and program increment (PI) planning.
- Work with Scrum Masters and Agile teams to break down features into smaller, incremental deliverables.
- Serve as the primary point of contact for API and webhook-related product decisions.

Knowledge, Skills, And Abilities
- Strong understanding of webhooks, APIs, event-driven architecture, and data platforms. Proven experience in backlog management, feature refinement, and sprint execution in an Agile environment
- Strong conflict resolution skills and ability to lead discussions with multiple stakeholders. Experience working with high-performing teams in a fast-paced, data-driven environment
- Excellent communication and collaboration skills, with the ability to influence cross-functional teams. Deep technical understanding of APIs, event-driven architecture, and data platforms
- Experience with Jira, Agility, ADO, or other similar backlog management and reporting tools. Capable of conducting all Agile standard ceremonies and interface with the Product Team's counterpart to align Product's requirements to Engineering stories.
- Ability to work with the Engineering team to create and refine stories for execution.
- Able to produce accurate project status and blockers for weekly management reports

Minimum Education
- Bachelor's degree or equivalency in Product Marketing, Economics, Business, Computer Science/Technologies or related field.

Minimum Experience
- Three to four (3-4) years of experience in Technical Product Management, Technical Product Ownership, or API/Product Development.

Salary Estimate: up to $185,000.
