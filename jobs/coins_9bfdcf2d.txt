Coins.ph is on a mission to create an open financial system by providing everyone easy access to Web3 and digital assets. A regulated entity, Coins is the most established crypto brand in the Philippines and has gained the trust of more than 16 million users. Through the easy-to-use mobile app, users can buy and sell a variety of different cryptocurrencies and also access a wide range of payment services.
Responsibilities
Product Strategy & Roadmap: Define and execute the product vision and roadmap for crypto trading solutions, aligning with company objectives and market trends.
Market Analysis: Conduct thorough market research to identify customer needs, emerging trends, and competitive offerings in the cryptocurrency space.
Cross-Functional Collaboration: Work closely with engineering, design, marketing, compliance, and customer support teams to ensure successful product development and launch.
User Experience: Collaborate with UX/UI teams to create intuitive and user-friendly trading interfaces that enhance customer engagement and satisfaction.
Performance Monitoring: Track and analyze product performance metrics, gather user feedback, and make data-driven decisions to optimize product offerings.
Regulatory Compliance: Ensure that all trading products comply with relevant regulations and industry standards.
Requirements
Experience: Minimum 5 years of proven experience in product management within the cryptocurrency or financial services industry, with a strong understanding of crypto trading platforms.
Technical Proficiency: Familiarity with blockchain technology and digital assets.
Analytical Skills: Strong analytical and problem-solving skills, with the ability to translate data insights into actionable product strategies.
Communication: Excellent verbal and written communication skills, capable of articulating complex concepts to a range of stakeholders.
Education: Bachelor's degree in Business, Computer Science, Finance, or a related field. An MBA or advanced degree is a plus.
