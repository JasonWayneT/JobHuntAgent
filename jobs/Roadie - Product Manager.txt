Company: Roadie
Title: Product Manager

Roadie, a UPS Company, is a crowdsourced delivery platform that connects businesses with items to send with drivers already on the road. Roadie is looking for a Product Manager to join our team and help us build the future of last-mile delivery.

Responsibilities:
* Own the product strategy and roadmap for your area of the Roadie platform.
* Conduct market research and user interviews to understand customer needs and identify new opportunities.
* Define and prioritize product requirements and work closely with engineering and design to build and launch new features and products.
* Analyze data and user feedback to measure the success of new features and products and identify areas for improvement.
* Communicate the product vision and strategy to stakeholders across the company.

Qualifications:
* 3+ years of experience in product management, preferably in a fast-paced, high-growth environment.
* Strong analytical skills and the ability to use data to drive product decisions.
* Excellent communication and collaboration skills.
* Passion for the delivery and logistics industry.
* Experience with crowdsourced platforms or marketplaces is a plus.
