# Product Manager - Remote at Braintrust (Taste)

- Product Direction & UX Strategy Take ownership of the product vision, with a clear focus on delivering a premium, mobile-first experience that leverages Taste’s AI models. Develop a deep understanding of the partner’s brand, licensing constraints, and optimal product UX. Identify critical paths to launch, ensuring the interface is intuitive, visually stunning, and true to the IP. Balance the ambition of the creative vision with practical delivery.
- User Journey & Solution Design Define the customer journey end-to-end (User Journey Mapping), ensuring the solution captivates users while meeting launch goals. Collaborate closely with UI/UX agencies, designers, and AI researchers to shape solutions that are delightful to use and technically viable.
- Roadmap Ownership and Prioritization Assert full product ownership of the application. Own the project schedule and backlog end-to-end, setting clear priorities aligned to the partner’s launch window. Enforce key decisions on feature prioritization, UI polish, and release schedules. Make disciplined scope decisions to ensure the most critical user-facing features are delivered without compromising the launch date.
- Delivery & Team Alignment Serve as the connector between creative intent and engineering progress of external and internal teams. Ensure sprint goals are clearly defined, design feedback is consolidated, and blockers are removed. Take accountability for the project’s successful deployment, ensuring the final product matches the approved design fidelity.
- Commercial and Go-To-Market Work closely with Taste leadership and marketing to ensure the web app supports the broader campaign strategy. Ensure the application is refined and ready for a high-profile public launch.
- Product Communication and Alignment Communicate project status, risks, and milestones clearly across Taste and the external partner stakeholders. Build alignment between creative teams, the technical team, and the client. Act as a strong product leader who brings clarity, structure, and momentum to a high-stakes, client-facing project.
